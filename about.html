ABOUT US

B. & A. ASSOCIATES

B. & A. Associates is a chartered accountancy firm registered in 2017 pursuant to Nepal Chartered Accountants Act, 1997 having registration number 707. B. & A. Associates provides Auditing & Assurance, Management Consulting, Risk Advisory, Taxation Advisory and Accountancy Services to its clients. B & A’s main intent is to provide innovate solutions to clients’ requirements through its professionals who strive for excellence. The knowledge base available with B & A helps it deliver with integrity and adding value to its clients.

B. & A. Associates has a strong team of 25 professionals comprising of 2 partners and 3 managers having experience of providing Assurance, Advisory and Accountancy services in Banking and Financial Institution Sector, Hospitality Industry, Manufacturing Industry, Hydropower Industry, Service Sector, Development Sector, Fund Management Companies, Education Sector and Trading Sector.

B. & A. Associates is based in Kathmandu Valley and is capable of providing its services all over Nepal.

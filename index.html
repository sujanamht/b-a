ABOUT B. & A. ASSOCIATES

B. & A. Associates is a distinguished chartered accountancy firm based in Kathmandu, Nepal, providing comprehensive financial solutions since 2017. With expertise in Auditing & Assurance, Risk Advisory, Taxation, Management Consulting, and Accountancy Services, B. & A. is committed to delivering innovative, ethical, and client-centric financial services. Backed by a team of highly skilled professionals, the firm serves diverse industries, including banking, hospitality, manufacturing, hydropower, and education, ensuring compliance, transparency, and strategic financial growth. Integrity, professionalism, and excellence define B. & A. Associates as a trusted partner in Nepal’s financial landscape.
